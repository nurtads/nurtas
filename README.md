        <h2>Key Principles</h2>
        <p>The law of conservation of energy states that in a closed system, the total energy remains constant over time, provided no external forces are acting within it.</p>
    </section>

    <section>
        <h2>Formula for Conservation of Energy</h2>
        <p>The mathematical expression for the law of conservation of energy is:</p>
        <p>E<sub>initial</sub> = E<sub>final</sub></p>
        <p>Where E<sub>initial</sub> is the initial energy of the system, and E<sub>final</sub> is the final energy of the system.</p>
